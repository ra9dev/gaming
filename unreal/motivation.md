## Prerequisites

- You are comfortable launching Unreal Engine and navigating the 3D viewport, including using the **Outliner** and **Details** panels.
- You are familiar with [fab.com](https://fab.com) and understand how to **integrate third-party assets** (such as static meshes) into your project.
- You can create **basic logic in Blueprints** to drive simple game mechanics.

In other words, if you are a literal potato who doesn't know how to start a new project in UE, go spend a few evenings learning the absolute basics and come back.
